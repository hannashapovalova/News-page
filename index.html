There will be a lot of "Bavovna": A satellite was bought with the money of Ukrainians

For 600 million hryvnias raised by Ukrainians, the Serhiy Prytula Foundation bought a satellite and access to the satellite database pictures for the Ukrainian army. It's not just one satellite. It gives us access to a database of satellite images of the constellation ICEYE also bought this access more than a year ahead. From now on, our army will receive high-quality satellite images as quickly as possible, which will help us with operational planning of operations. There will be a lot of "Bavovna".
